About Our Project

In the evolving landscape of cloud computing, ensuring end-to-end security has become a pressing concern. Our project, Secure Outsourcing and Sharing of Cloud Data with User Anonymity and Client-Side Encryption, pioneers a sophisticated, hybrid cryptographic framework integrating AES-128, and Identity-Based Encryption for resilient cloud interactions.

By executing client-side encryption and deploying for file integrity validation, we ensure that user data remains tamper-proof and verifiable even across distributed systems.

Leveraging Node.js, Express, and MySQL at the backend, fortified with JWT-based authentication and SSL/TLS secured APIs, our platform delivers unmatched security, scalability, and throughput.
